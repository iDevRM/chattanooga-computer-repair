Computer Repair Services in Chattanooga, TN

If you don't see your issue listed, call us — there's a good chance we can help.

🦠 Virus & Malware Removal

If your computer is running slow, showing weird pop-ups, or doing things on its own, there's a good chance something's infected it. We scan, clean, and secure your machine so it runs the way it should. We'll also show you how to avoid it happening again.

🐢 Slow Computer Tune-Up

Computers slow down over time — it's normal, but it's fixable. We dig into what's actually causing the slowdown, whether it's too many startup programs, a clogged hard drive, outdated software, or something else. Most people are surprised how much faster their old machine can run.

💻 Laptop Screen Replacement

Cracked screens happen. We replace screens on most laptop brands — Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, MacBook, and more. Drop it off, and we'll usually have it back to you the same day or next day.

💾 Data Recovery

Hard drive died? Accidentally deleted something important? We use professional recovery tools to get your files back in a lot of situations. Photos, documents, videos — we work carefully to recover what matters to you.

⬆ Hardware Upgrades (RAM & SSD)

Adding more RAM or swapping to a solid-state drive is one of the best things you can do for an older computer. It's fast, affordable, and makes a huge difference. We source the right parts for your machine and install them while you wait if you'd like.

🔄 Operating System Reinstall

Sometimes the cleanest fix is starting fresh. We back up your files, wipe the drive, reinstall Windows or macOS, and get everything set back up. Your machine comes back running like new.

⌨ Keyboard Repair

Keys sticking, keys not working, keyboard completely dead — we can fix or replace keyboards on most laptops. Desktop keyboard problems are usually even simpler to sort out.

🔋 Battery Replacement

If your laptop won't hold a charge or dies in an hour, it's probably just the battery. We carry or can source replacement batteries for most models and swap them out quickly.

💧 Water Damage Repair

Spilled something on your computer? Turn it off immediately and bring it in as soon as possible — time really matters with water damage. We clean the board and assess what's salvageable. We've recovered plenty of machines people thought were total losses.

📶 Network & WiFi Troubleshooting

Can't get online? WiFi dropping constantly? Slow internet even though your speeds should be fine? We troubleshoot network issues on the computer side and can help you figure out if the problem is your machine or your router.

🍎 Mac Repair

Everything listed above applies to Macs too. MacBook screen replacements, Mac tune-ups, macOS reinstalls, data recovery — we handle it all. Both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs.

🏢 Business Computer Services

Got multiple computers at your office? We work with small businesses in Chattanooga on everything from setup and maintenance to fixing whatever breaks. Ask us about ongoing support plans.

Not Sure What's Wrong? That's Fine.

Bring it in and we'll run a free diagnosis. No pressure, no obligation.
