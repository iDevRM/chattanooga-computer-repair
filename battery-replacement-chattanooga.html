Why Do Laptop Batteries Degrade in Chattanooga?

Laptop batteries in Chattanooga degrade through normal use — lithium-ion batteries have a finite number of charge cycles before their capacity permanently decreases. Leaving a laptop plugged in at 100% for extended periods, exposing it to heat (like leaving it in a hot car during a Chattanooga summer), and running the battery down to zero regularly all accelerate degradation. Most laptop batteries start to show noticeable capacity loss after 2–3 years of regular use.
