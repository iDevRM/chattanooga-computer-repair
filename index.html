Common Questions

Straight answers — no tech speak.

How much does computer repair cost in Chattanooga?

Most common repairs run between $50 and $150. Things like virus removal, tune-ups, or a RAM upgrade are usually on the lower end. Screen replacements and data recovery can be a bit more. We give you a price upfront before we touch anything — no surprise bills.

How long will my repair take?

A lot of repairs are done the same day you drop it off. Virus removal, software issues, and basic hardware swaps usually take a few hours. Screen replacements and data recovery can take a day or two. We'll give you a realistic timeline when you come in.

Do you fix both PCs and Macs?

Yes — both. Windows desktops, Windows laptops, MacBooks, iMacs. If it's a computer, we can work on it.

What if you can't fix it?

If we can't fix it, you don't pay for the repair. We'll tell you what's wrong and give you an honest opinion on whether it's worth fixing. Sometimes it's not — and we'll tell you that too.

Can you recover my files if my computer crashed?

Often yes. Whether it's a hard drive failure, accidental deletion, or a corrupted drive, we have tools to recover data in a lot of situations. The sooner you bring it in after a crash, the better the chances.

Is my data safe with you?

Absolutely. We don't access your personal files unless it's directly related to the repair. Everything stays confidential.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes — all our repairs come with a 30-day warranty. If the same issue comes back within 30 days, we fix it again at no charge.
